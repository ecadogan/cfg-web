Get organised, save time and focus on teaching with our app.

Dog Ate My Lesson Plan is a compreghensive tool that simplifies lesson planning process for teachers, helps them stay organised and on track, and supports them in delivering high-quality education to their students.

Lesson Plans

The app is a centralised location for teachers to store and organise their lesson plans, making it easy to access and review at any time.

Curriculum Links

The app is designed to integrate seamlessly with the Welsh Curriculum, making it easy for teachers to ensure their lessons are aligned with national standards.

Easy Pupil Assessment

The app includes tools for tracking student progress and assessing their understanding of key concepts, allowing teachers to adjust their lessons and teaching methods as needed.
